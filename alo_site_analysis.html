Opportunity Score Adjustment

The Opportunity Score adjusts the raw Composite Score downward for markets where Alo already has significant penetration relative to Lululemon. The adjustment formula uses an Alo saturation ratio (existing Alo stores / Lululemon stores) to apply a penalty of up to 25%. This surfaces greenfield markets — where the affluent Alo customer clearly exists (validated by Lululemon presence) but Alo hasn't yet captured them.
